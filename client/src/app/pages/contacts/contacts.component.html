Experimental Pathology Facility

Institute of Oncology Research (IOR) — Bellinzona, Switzerland

A centralized core facility providing integrated histopathology and spatial proteomics services to support tissue-based research with standardized workflows and expert consultation.

Spatial Proteomics Unit

The unit offers high-dimensional tissue imaging using the MACSima™ cyclic immunofluorescence platform, including experimental design consultation, sample preparation optimization, image acquisition, data quality control, downstream analysis, tissue microarray projects, antibody conjugation, and access to a curated antibody repository.
